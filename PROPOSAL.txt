================================================================================
PROJECT PROPOSAL: CANDI NUSANTARA
Digital Preservation & Interactive Exploration of Indonesia's Ancient Heritage
================================================================================

1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
---------------------
Candi Nusantara is a premium, high-impact web platform designed to preserve and 
showcase the architectural and historical grandeur of Indonesia's ancient temples 
(Candi). The project bridges the gap between historical scholarship and modern 
digital storytelling, offering an immersive educational experience that 
celebrates the "Warisan Peradaban Nusantara" (Nusantara's Civilizational Heritage).

2. CORE VISION & PURPOSE
-------------------------
- DIGITAL PRESERVATION: Creating a robust digital archive of 11+ major temples 
  using high-resolution local assets and 3D models.
- INTERACTIVE EDUCATION: Moving beyond static text to dynamic, scroll-triggered 
  narratives that engage the digital-native generation.
- CULTURAL ACCESSIBILITY: Providing a centralized hub for cross-regional 
  temple distribution, historical filtering, and dynastic context.

3. DESIGN SYSTEM: "BATU KUNO" (ANCIENT STONE)
---------------------------------------------
The platform employs a bespoke, premium aesthetic tailored to the subject matter:
- PALETTE: Slate Dark (#0A0806), Heirloom Gold (#C8A951), and Aged Parchment (#F5ECD7).
- TYPOGRAPHY: 'Cinzel' for headers (evoking stone inscriptions) and 'Outfit' 
  for body text (modern readability).
- AESTHETIC: Glassmorphism, smooth gradients, and cinematic text-reveal effects.

4. KEY PLATFORM FEATURES
-------------------------
- CINEMATIC HERO STORYTELLING: A multi-phase, scroll-driven interactive sequence 
  introducing the spirit of the archipelago.
- INTERACTIVE 3D ARTIFACTS: High-fidelity Sketchfab 3D viewer integration 
  allowing users to manipulate and inspect temple structures.
- GEOGRAPHIC DISTRIBUTION: Custom Leaflet-based interactive map showing 
  temple locations from Sumatra to Bali.
- DYNAMIC DATA FILTERING: Seamless categorization by Era (7th-15th century), 
  Religion (Hindu/Buddhist), Island, and Dynasty (Sailendra, Sanjaya, Majapahit, etc.).
- SCROLL-BASED TEXT REVEAL: Sophisticated animations that unveil historical 
  narratives as the user explores.

5. TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE
--------------------------
- FRAMEWORK: Next.js 15+ (App Router) for high-performance SSR and SEO.
- ANIMATION ENGINE: Framer Motion for kinetic UI and Lenis for smooth scrolling.
- ASSET MANAGEMENT: Fully self-contained implementation with local 
  high-resolution photography to ensure production reliability.
- SEO & PWA: Optimized metadata, Sitemap.xml, Robots.txt, and PWA manifest for 
  cross-platform discoverability.

6. TARGET AUDIENCE & IMPACT
----------------------------
- EDUCATION: Students and researchers exploring Indonesian history in 3D.
- TOURISM: International and local travelers planning heritage visits.
- CULTURE: Heritage enthusiasts seeking a premium, curated digital museum.

7. CONCLUSION
--------------
Candi Nusantara is more than a website; it is an interactive tribute to human 
ingenuity. By combining state-of-the-art web technology with deep historical 
narratives, it sets a new standard for cultural preservation in the digital age.
